研究方向 Research Interests

生态系统服务 Ecosystem Services

研究生态系统服务的时空演变格局、交互关系及其驱动机制，探索生态环境质量与景观格局的动态关联。 Studying spatiotemporal evolution patterns, interactions, and driving mechanisms of ecosystem services, exploring dynamic relationships between ecological environmental quality and landscape patterns.

可持续发展 Sustainable Development

关注区域可持续发展问题，研究耕地利用生态效率、绿色金融与乡村发展的耦合协调关系。 Focusing on regional sustainable development, studying cultivated land use eco-efficiency and the coupled coordination between green finance and rural development.

社会-生态系统 Social-Ecological Systems

从社会-生态系统耦合视角出发，研究人地关系的非线性效应与时空异质性。 Studying nonlinear effects and spatiotemporal heterogeneity of human-environment relationships from the perspective of coupled social-ecological systems.

AI for Science AI for Science

探索人工智能技术在地理科学与生态环境研究中的应用，推动数据驱动的科学发现。 Exploring applications of artificial intelligence in geographical science and ecological research, promoting data-driven scientific discovery.
